Qi Teng is currently an Associate Researcher (直聘副研究员) at the School of Management, Zhengzhou University, and received the Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University. His recent work focuses on sensor-based human activity recognition, efficient neural architectures, attention-driven time-series modeling, wearable computing, and data-driven nonlinear dynamical systems.
