Qi Teng

滕起

Associate Researcher (直聘副研究员) working on wearable sensing, time-series modeling, and human activity recognition.

Qi Teng is currently with the School of Information Management at Zhengzhou University and received the Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University. Current research focuses on sensor-based human activity recognition, efficient neural architectures, temporal and spatial attention modeling, wearable computing, and data-driven nonlinear dynamical systems.
