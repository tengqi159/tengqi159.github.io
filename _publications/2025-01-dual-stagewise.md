## Abstract

This paper proposes a dual stage-wise decoupling network architecture for addressing long-tailed distribution in wearable sensor-based activity recognition. The two-stage approach effectively handles both representation learning and classifier training for imbalanced HAR datasets.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed dual stage-wise decoupling for long-tailed HAR
- Published in top pattern recognition journal
- Effective handling of extreme class imbalance
