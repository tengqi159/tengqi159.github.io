## Abstract

This paper investigates the effectiveness of using smaller convolutional filters combined with layer-wise training for human activity recognition. The proposed approach achieves competitive accuracy while significantly reducing computational complexity.

## Key Contributions

- Demonstrated effectiveness of smaller filter sizes for HAR
- Combined with layer-wise training for improved efficiency
- Reduced model complexity while maintaining accuracy
