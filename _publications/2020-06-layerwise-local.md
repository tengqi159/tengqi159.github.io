## Abstract

This paper proposes a layer-wise training strategy using local loss functions for convolutional neural networks in sensor-based human activity recognition. The approach enables efficient training and improved feature learning at each layer, resulting in better recognition performance.

## Key Contributions

- Introduced local loss functions for layer-wise CNN training
- Demonstrated improved feature learning for HAR
- Achieved significant accuracy improvements on benchmark datasets
