## Abstract

Human activity recognition (HAR) using wearable sensors has attracted significant attention in various applications. In this paper, we propose DanHAR, a novel dual attention network for multimodal human activity recognition. The model introduces channel attention and temporal attention mechanisms to capture both sensor-wise and time-wise dependencies in multi-channel time series data.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed a dual attention mechanism combining channel and temporal attention
- Achieved state-of-the-art results on multiple HAR benchmark datasets
- Demonstrated effective multimodal sensor fusion
