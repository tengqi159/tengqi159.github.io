## Abstract

This paper proposes RepHAR, a novel re-parameterizable architecture for human activity recognition that enables flexible accuracy-speed tradeoff during deployment. The network can be decoupled for efficient inference while maintaining high recognition accuracy.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed re-parameterizable architecture for HAR
- Enables accuracy-speed tradeoff for deployment
- Efficient inference on edge devices
