## Abstract

This paper proposes CSFO, a category-specific flattening optimization method to address the long-tailed distribution problem in sensor-based activity recognition. The approach effectively handles class imbalance in HAR datasets.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed category-specific optimization for long-tailed HAR
- Published in top-tier ACM CIKM conference
- Addresses practical class imbalance challenges
