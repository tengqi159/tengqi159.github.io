## Abstract

This paper proposes a triple cross-domain attention mechanism that captures dependencies across temporal, spatial, and channel domains for wearable sensor-based human activity recognition. The proposed approach effectively models complex inter-dependencies in multi-channel time series data.

## Key Contributions

- Proposed triple attention across temporal, spatial, and channel domains
- Published in top-tier journal IEEE TNNLS
- State-of-the-art performance on HAR benchmarks
